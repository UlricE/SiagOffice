Keybindings

Here are the standard keybindings that are available when the stage is visible.

H
Pop down the stage.
O
Pop down the stage and open another file.
Q
Quit the program.
S
Stop the animation.
P
Move to previous animation frame.
N
Move to next animation frame.
C
Play the animation.
Space
Move to the next slide.
Backspace
Move to the prevous slide.
